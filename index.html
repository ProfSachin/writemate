What's inside

The tools

Four interactive utilities and two info pages. Each one does a small, specific job for typing or writing.

01.

Typing Speed Test

Pick 15, 30, or 60 seconds and start typing. Your WPM, accuracy and character count update as you go.

02.

Keyboard Practice

Drills for home row, top row, numbers and common letter pairs. The on-screen keyboard shows you which key is next.

03.

Writing Tools

Word counter, case converter, text transformer and a lorem ipsum generator. The little jobs that come up when you're writing.

04.

Essay Templates

Six ready-made structures: argumentative essay, lab report, cover letter and more. Fill the inputs, get a draft.

05.

Built-in Stats

Words, sentences, paragraphs and reading time, shown wherever you'd want to see them. No sign-up.

06.

Runs in your browser

No server and no account. The whole thing works the same way offline as online.
