Writing advice that actually helps

A · Read aloud

Find awkward sentences

Anything you stumble on while reading aloud is something a reader will stumble on too. Rewrite it.

B · Cut 10%

Trim every draft

Aim to delete one word in ten on your second pass. The argument almost always gets sharper.

C · One idea

One idea per paragraph

If you can't summarise a paragraph in one sentence, you've packed too much in. Split it.
