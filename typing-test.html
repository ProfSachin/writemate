A few tips

  • Try not to look at the keyboard. Looking down to find a key is what slows most people down.
  • Keep your wrists relaxed. If your shoulders are tense, your fingers will be too.
  • Accuracy first. A high WPM with mistakes is slower than a steady, clean pace.
  • Short, regular practice. Ten minutes a day works better than one long session a week.
