Where your fingers go

Your fingers rest on the home row. Left hand on A S D F and right hand on J K L ;. Both index fingers should be able to feel the small bumps on F and J, which is how you find the row without looking.

Left hand

Pinky: A, Q, Z
Ring: S, W, X
Middle: D, E, C
Index: F, R, V (also G, T, B)

Right hand

Index: J, U, M (also H, Y, N)
Middle: K, I, ,
Ring: L, O, .
Pinky: ;, P, /
